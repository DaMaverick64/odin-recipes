Ogbono Soup

Description

Ogbono soup is native to the western part of Nigeria. It is a soup that has a stretchy consistency with a smooth travel down the throat.

Ingredients

  • Ogbono seeds
  • Onion
  • Chili
  • Seasoning and Salt
  • Palm Oil
  • Protein(Beef, Fish, Stockfish etc)
  • Pumpkin leaeves

Steps

  1. Bring Water(Quantity determined by quantity of Ogbono) to warm temperature.
  2. Pour in already simmered protein into water and add seasoning, salt and chili and bring to boil.
  3. Mix ogbono powder with oil till it has a pasty consistency.
  4. Drop Ogbono paste into Soup consistency and cook till it tastes and smells cooked.
  5. pour grated leaves into soup and turn for a short while.
  6. Remove from heat source and serve with Semolina moulds.
